The Board of Trustees and service providers who manage and protect your pension

Board of Trustees

The DFRRF is governed by a seven-member Board of Trustees responsible for managing fund assets, setting investment policy, and ensuring benefits are paid as promised. The Board meets the third Wednesday of each month at 8:30 AM at Central Fire Station (332 E. Hickory St., Denton). Meetings are open to all members.
