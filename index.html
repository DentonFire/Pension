6
Contribution Summary
You contribute 12.6% of your pay toward your pension. Separately, the City of Denton contributes 18.5% of your compensation on your behalf. The City's contribution is not deducted from your paycheck.
7
Income Replacement
Financial planners generally recommend replacing 70% to 85% of pre-retirement income. Your DFRRF benefit covers a significant share. The gap can be bridged with the IAFF 457 plan, personal savings, or Social Security (if eligible).

Benefit estimates are for informational purposes only. Salary data is from the FY 2024-2025 Civil Service Fire Pay Plan (effective January 4, 2025) and does not reflect actual payroll records.
