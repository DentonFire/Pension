Resources

Documents, reports, and updates that keep you connected to your retirement

Governing Documents

These are the primary documents that define how the fund operates, how benefits are calculated, and how investments are managed. If you ever want to verify a specific provision, these are the authoritative sources.

PDF
Plan Document
The official benefit provisions, including the 2.59% benefit rate (Section B.2), retirement eligibility, vesting, and survivor benefits. Available through the member portal.
PDF
Investment Policy Statement
Asset allocation targets, risk parameters, and performance benchmarks that guide how fund assets are invested. Last updated November 2020.
PDF
Meet and Confer Agreement (2023)
Ordinance 2023-1643. Codifies the 18.5% city contribution floor, 6.75% assumed rate of return, and closed 25-year amortization target.
PDF
TLFFRA (Article 6243e)
The Texas Local Fire Fighters Retirement Act. The state law that authorizes our fund and 41 other fire pension systems in Texas.

Reports & Studies

Actuarial valuations, experience studies, and regulatory filings that track the fund's financial health over time.

PDF
2026 TLFFRA Report
PRB report covering all 42 TLFFRA systems. Includes Denton's funded ratio, amortization period, and peer comparisons.
Need a specific document? Many of these are available through the member portal. If you can't find what you're looking for, contact Gary Calmes at gary@dentonfirepension.com and he'll get you a copy.

Pension Newsletter

The DFRRF Board of Trustees publishes a regular newsletter to keep members informed about the fund. Each issue covers how the fund is performing, what the Board is working on, and what members should be doing to stay on top of their retirement benefits.

Whether you just finished the academy or you're counting down your last few shifts, these updates are written for you. No jargon, no filler. Just the information that matters for your career and your family's financial future.
